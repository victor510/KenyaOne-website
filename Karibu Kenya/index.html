Kenya

-12'th December, 1963

Kenya is a country in East Africa with her coastline on the Indian Ocean. It encompasses the savannah, lakelands, the dramatic Great Rift Valley and mountain highlands. It's also home to wildlife like lions elephants and rihnos. From Nairobi, the capital, safaris visit the Maasai Mara Reserve, known for its annual wildebeest migrations and Amboseli National Park, offering a view of Tanzania's 5,895m Mt. Kilimanjaro.
